Veg Biryani

Veg Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish cooked with aromatic spices and a mix of vegetables. It's a beloved staple in Indian cuisine, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Long-grain Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (bay leaf, cumin, cardamom, cloves)
  • Coriander and mint leaves
  • Ghee or oil

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 10g
