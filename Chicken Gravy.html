Chicken Gravy

Chicken Gravy is a flavorful and rich dish made with tender pieces of chicken simmered in a thick, spicy gravy. It's a staple in Indian households, often served with rice or bread.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala)
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 25g
