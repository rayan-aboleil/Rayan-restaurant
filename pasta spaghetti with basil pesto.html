Pasta Spaghetti with Basil Pesto

Indulge in our exquisite Pasta Spaghetti with Basil Pesto, a dish that perfectly combines the rich flavors of fresh basil, garlic, and parmesan cheese. This classic Italian dish is a favorite for its simplicity and delightful taste.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Garlic cloves
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pine nuts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 55g
  • Fat: 18g
