Chicken and Vegetables Platter

The Chicken and Vegetables Platter is a wholesome and colorful dish featuring perfectly cooked chicken paired with a variety of fresh vegetables. This platter is not only nutritious but also visually appealing, making it a great choice for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Zucchini
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices (thyme, garlic, pepper)

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 40g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Fat: 15g
