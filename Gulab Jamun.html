Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a traditional Indian dessert made from deep-fried dough balls soaked in a sweet syrup flavored with rose water and cardamom. Its soft and syrupy texture makes it a favorite at festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • Milk powder
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Sugar
  • Rose water
  • Cardamom powder
  • Water

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 2g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 5g
