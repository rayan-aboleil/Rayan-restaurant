Pepperoni Pizza

Indulge in our classic Pepperoni Pizza, a timeless favorite among pizza lovers. Featuring a perfectly baked crust, rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and generous slices of spicy pepperoni, this dish is sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano and basil

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 36g
  • Fat: 15g
