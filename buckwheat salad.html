Buckwheat Salad

This refreshing buckwheat salad is a delightful mix of healthy ingredients, featuring cherry tomatoes, red onion, and fresh herbs. It's a perfect light dish for any occasion, packed with nutrients and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Buckwheat
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 180
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 5g
