Subway-style Sandwiches

Enjoy our delicious Subway-style sandwiches, featuring a variety of fresh ingredients stacked high between soft, crusty bread. Perfect for a satisfying lunch or a quick snack, these sandwiches are both versatile and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh bread (sub rolls or baguette)
  • Turkey or chicken slices
  • Fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, onions)
  • Cheese (your choice)
  • Condiments (mayo, mustard, sauces)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 15g
