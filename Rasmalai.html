Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a delicious Indian dessert made from soft paneer cheese balls soaked in a creamy and sweet milk sauce. It's flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts, making it a perfect treat for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Almonds and pistachios for garnishing
  • Rose water (optional)

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
