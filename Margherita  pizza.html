Margherita Pizza

Enjoy our classic Margherita Pizza, featuring a delightful blend of fresh mozzarella cheese, aromatic basil, and rich tomato sauce on a perfectly baked crust. This simple yet flavorful pizza captures the essence of traditional Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
