Blend Cocktails with Orange Fruit

Enjoy a refreshing blend cocktail featuring a delightful mix of fresh orange juice and other fruity flavors. Perfect for any occasion, this cocktail is both vibrant and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh orange juice
  • Vodka or rum (optional)
  • Other fruit juices (like pineapple or cranberry)
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh fruit slices for garnish
