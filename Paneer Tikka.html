Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) that is grilled or roasted. It is known for its smoky flavor and is often served with mint chutney.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Garam Masala)
  • Vegetables (Bell Peppers, Onions)
  • Mint Chutney for serving

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Fat: 20g
