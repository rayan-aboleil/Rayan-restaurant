Grilled Salmon Fillet

This grilled salmon fillet is a delightful dish, known for its rich flavor and healthy profile. It is cooked to perfection and served with a fresh vegetable salad, making it a perfect choice for a nutritious meal.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillet
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh herbs (dill, parsley)
  • Mixed salad greens

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Fat: 25g
