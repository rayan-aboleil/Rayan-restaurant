Turkey Tomato Sub Sandwiches

Delight in our Turkey Tomato Sub Sandwiches, made with fresh turkey breast, ripe tomatoes, and crisp lettuce all nestled in a soft sub roll. Perfect for lunch or a quick snack, these sandwiches are both satisfying and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh turkey breast
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Sub roll
  • Mayonnaise or mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 24g
  • Carbohydrates: 38g
  • Fat: 8g
