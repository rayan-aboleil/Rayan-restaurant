Tiramisu

Indulge in our classic Tiramisu, a delightful Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and rich mascarpone cheese. Topped with a dusting of cocoa powder, this dessert is a perfect blend of flavors and textures, sure to please any palate.

Ingredients:

  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Espresso coffee
  • Sugar
  • Ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla extract

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 16g
