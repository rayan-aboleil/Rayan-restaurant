Salad with Fish

This vibrant salad combines fresh greens with delicious baked salmon, cherry tomatoes, and a zesty lime dressing. It's not only visually appealing but also packed with nutrients, making it an excellent choice for a healthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh greens (lettuce, spinach)
  • Baked salmon
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 18g
