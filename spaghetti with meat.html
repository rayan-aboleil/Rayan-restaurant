Spaghetti with Meat

Indulge in our delicious Spaghetti with Meat, featuring tender spaghetti noodles topped with a rich and savory meat sauce. This classic Italian dish is a favorite among food lovers, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Italian herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 15g
