Chicken Lollipop

Chicken Lollipop is a popular Indian appetizer that consists of chicken wings that are cut and shaped to resemble a lollipop. They are marinated in a mix of spices and then deep-fried to perfection, resulting in a crispy exterior and juicy interior.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Wings
  • Garlic Paste
  • Ginger Paste
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 30g
