Grilled Steak

This delicious grilled steak is marinated to perfection and grilled over high heat to achieve a juicy, tender finish. Served with a side of fresh vegetables, it is a classic dish that never disappoints.

Ingredients:

  • Steak (ribeye, sirloin, or your choice)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetables for grilling (bell peppers, zucchini)

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 40g
  • Carbohydrates: 0g
  • Fat: 30g
