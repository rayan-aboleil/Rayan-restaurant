Baked Eggplant

Baked Eggplant is a delicious and healthy dish made from fresh eggplants baked to perfection with tomatoes, garlic, and spices. This flavorful dish can be served as a main course or a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplants
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh herbs (basil or parsley)

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 120
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 8g
