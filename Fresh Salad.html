Fresh Salad

This fresh salad is a delightful mix of crisp vegetables, herbs, and a light dressing, providing a refreshing start to any meal. It's packed with nutrients and is perfect for a healthy lifestyle.

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red onion
  • Bell peppers
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 12g
