Tuna Sandwich

Enjoy our delicious Tuna Sandwich, made with freshly prepared tuna salad, crisp lettuce, and ripe tomatoes, all nestled between two slices of toasted bread. It's a perfect blend of flavors, providing a satisfying and nutritious meal for any time of day.

Ingredients:

  • Canned tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Whole grain bread

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 15g
