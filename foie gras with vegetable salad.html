Foie Gras with Vegetable Salad

Foie Gras with Vegetable Salad is a luxurious dish featuring rich and buttery foie gras served alongside a refreshing salad of seasonal vegetables. This elegant pairing brings together flavors and textures for a gourmet experience.

Ingredients:

  • Foie gras
  • Mixed greens (arugula, spinach)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Nutritional Information (per serving):

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fat: 28g
